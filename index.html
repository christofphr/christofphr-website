PROFILE

Originally from Oklahoma, Chris is based in New York, where he works in finance. He’s drawn to warm lighting and places with texture: the city, a car, a pool table... rooms where something just happened or is about to.

His work lives in the tension between grit and restraint—balancing what's worn-in with what's been carefully composed. He photographs with an appreciation for places that feel lived-in, even when they're impeccably curated.
